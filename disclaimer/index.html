3. Calculator Inputs Stay on Your Device

Herminox is a fully client-side static website for its calculators. There are no servers receiving calculator inputs, no databases storing your margins or purchases, and no user accounts.

What this means in practice: Every calculation you perform runs entirely inside your own browser. The numbers you enter into calculators never leave your device through our application code. We cannot see your inputs or results.

The only exception to fully ephemeral calculator data is the Return & Capital Dashboard, which stores return entries in your browser's own localStorage — a private storage area on your device that Herminox servers cannot access. Clearing your browser's site data removes all stored entries permanently.

Herminox does not:

  • Collect, transmit, or store calculator inputs on any server
  • Require account registration or login of any kind
  • Interact with Amazon's API or Seller Central on your behalf
  • Monetize user data or sell information to third parties
  • Embed advertising pixels (Meta Pixel, Hotjar, or similar)

We do use Google Analytics (GA4) for aggregate page-traffic measurement. That measures visits to pages — not the numbers you type into calculators. Details are in our Privacy Policy. External resources also include Google Fonts (loaded as a performance-optional stylesheet).
