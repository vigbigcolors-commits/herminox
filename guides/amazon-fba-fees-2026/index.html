Amazon FBA fees in 2026: the three layers that decide your margin

Most “bad products” are fee products. Before you place a purchase order, you need referral, fulfillment, and the fuel surcharge in one model — not a vibe-based margin.

Worked example

Worked example. A $28 standard-size item, $9 landed cost, 15% referral ($4.20), ~$5.40 fulfillment. Fuel at 3.5% adds ~$0.19. Contribution before ads ≈ $28 − $9 − $4.20 − $5.40 − $0.19 = $9.21 (~33% margin). That is pre-PPC — not take-home.

Why “FBA fee” is three numbers, not one

Seller Central payouts feel like a black box because Amazon stacks fees. Referral is a percentage of the selling price (and can include shipping/gift wrap in the base). Fulfillment is a flat-ish per-unit charge from size and weight. The fuel surcharge rides on top of fulfillment — small per unit, loud at 1,000 units.

If you only model referral, compact heavy items look fine until fulfillment eats the rest. If you ignore fuel, your spreadsheet quietly drifts ~3–4% on fulfillment every month.

What still is not in a simple pre-launch model

Storage, inbound placement, returns processing, and aged inventory surcharges matter at scale. For a go/no-go sourcing decision, referral + fulfillment + fuel usually decide viability. Add storage later when velocity is real — do not skip the first three.

  • Confirm final fees in your own Seller Central fee preview.
  • Re-run numbers when Amazon publishes a new rate card.
  • Treat ad spend as a separate ceiling (ACoS breakeven), not as “misc.”

Checklist

  • Sell price and landed cost entered
  • Correct category (watch tiered categories)
  • Packaged weight and dimensions for size tier
  • Fuel surcharge toggled on if active
  • Margin still healthy after a realistic ACoS

FAQ

Are 2026 FBA fees the same in every marketplace?

No. This guide focuses on published US logic. UK/EU/other marketplaces use different schedules — always use the marketplace you sell in.

Does free shipping remove the referral fee?

No. Referral is generally calculated on the total customer payment components Amazon defines for that category — free shipping does not make referral vanish.

Should I include PPC in the FBA fee calculator?

Model fees and product cost first, then set an ad ceiling with breakeven ACoS. Mixing ads into “fees” hides which lever is broken.
