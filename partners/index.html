What “manual outreach” means

It is a daily habit of sending 5–10 personal emails to people who already teach Amazon sellers or buyers. You offer something useful (a free FBA / ACoS widget), not a pitch deck. They keep their audience; readers get a calculator; you get referral traffic via UTM tags.

  1. Find a fit. Amazon coaches, PPC consultants, FBA newsletters, seller YouTubers with a blog, buyer-deal sites. Public contact page or “press / collab” email only — no scraped Telegram lists.
  2. Open their site. Note one specific article or episode you actually liked. Personal line first; template second.
  3. Send one short email. Subject + 6–10 lines. Link herminox.com/embed/. Offer the snippet below. No attachment, no login ask.
  4. Track lightly. Spreadsheet: date, name, site, sent?, replied?, embedded? Check GA for utm_source from partners later.
  5. Follow up once. After 7–10 days, one polite bump. Then stop. No sequences of 8 emails.
